## Rules

- Always pair `teal-500` with `hover:teal-600` for interactive elements
- Use `emerald-500` for positive PnL, confirmed states, and success messages
- Use `red-400` for losses and errors (slightly softer than `red-500` to reduce alarm fatigue)
- Use `amber-400` for warnings, pending states, and TBA labels
